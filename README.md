Serves as the front-end for the application `Usain`, providing users with a GUI to access and interface with the provided methods: Uploading videos of their running, comparing it with top runners, and receiving AI (Google MediaPipe) powered insights into their form and posture, making running coaches more accessible, more objective, and more helpful for people worldwide. The users can do this on a frame-by-frame basis or for the whole video, allowing the opportunity to access their most granular data.
